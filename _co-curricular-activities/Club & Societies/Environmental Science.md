# Vision
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">For every student to be an Environment Champion.
	

# Mission
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">To promote the awareness of protecting the environment.
	
# Objectives
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">The Environmental Science Club aims to promote environmental awareness and care for the environment – both the school environment and our neighbourhood environment among our students.

# Approach
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">Students to be able to apply their knowledge, skills and values and take action to preserve, protect and improve the environment around them.
	

# Key Programmes
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">
<ul>
	<li>World Water Day Celebrations</li>
	<li>Earth Day Celebrations</li>
	<li>Upcycling Activities</li>
	<li>Gardening</li>
	</ul>
