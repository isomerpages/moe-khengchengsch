<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>For every student to be an Environment Champion.</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To promote the awareness of protecting the environment.</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>The Environmental Science Club aims to promote environmental awareness
and care for the environment – both the school environment and our neighbourhood
environment among our students.</p>
<h3>Approach</h3>
<p>Students to be able to apply their knowledge, skills and values and take
action to preserve, protect and improve the environment around them.</p>
