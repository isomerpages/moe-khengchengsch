### Vision
For every student to be an Environment Champion.
	

### Mission
To promote the awareness of protecting the environment.
	
### Objectives
The Environmental Science Club aims to promote environmental awareness and care for the environment – both the school environment and our neighbourhood environment among our students.

### Approach
Students to be able to apply their knowledge, skills and values and take action to preserve, protect and improve the environment around them.
	

### Key Programmes

* World Water Day Celebrations
* Earth Day Celebrations
* Upcycling Activities
* Gardening
