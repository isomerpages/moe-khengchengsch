<h1>Vision</h1>
<p>An Active Appreciator and Innovator of the Arts.</p>
<h1>Mission</h1>
<p>Empowering every child with the skills and knowledge to explore and experiment
in Art.</p>
<h1>Objectives &amp; Approach</h1>
<p>The Art &amp; Crafts Club curriculum seeks to teach our students to learn
and appreciate Art through the ‘3E’ approach.</p>
