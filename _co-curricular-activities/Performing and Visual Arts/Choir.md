<h1>Vision</h1>
<p>Every Child Can Learn to Sing</p>
<h1>Mission</h1>
<p>In Kheng Cheng School Choir, we believe that every child can learn to
sing given the right motivation, encouragement, good vocal training, as
well as stage presence to promote confidence in audience engagement.</p>
<h1>Objectives</h1>
<p>The KCS Choir aims to engage all segments of the school community to love
and be inclined to singing, and share the passion with the rest of the
world.</p>
<h1>Approach</h1>
<p>The KCS Choir practices every Fridays during CCA, under the guidance of
Ms Tan Ping Chuen. The Kodály Approach is used for students learning to
sharpen their singing aptitude. With a good blend of singing and movement,
students find joy in learning to sing in the Choir.</p>
