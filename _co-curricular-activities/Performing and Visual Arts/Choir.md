# Vision
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">Every Child Can Learn to Sing</span>

# Mission
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">In Kheng Cheng School Choir, we believe that every child can learn to sing given the right motivation, encouragement, good vocal training, as well as stage presence to promote confidence in audience engagement. </span>

# Objectives
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">The KCS Choir aims to engage all segments of the school community to love and be inclined to singing, and share the passion with the rest of the world.</span>

# Approach
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">The KCS Choir practices every Fridays during CCA, under the guidance of Ms Tan Ping Chuen. The Kodály Approach is used for students learning to sharpen their singing aptitude. With a good blend of singing and movement, students find joy in learning to sing in the Choir.</span>

# Key Programmes
<span style="font-size:16.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black">
<ul>
	<li>Chinese New Year Celebration</li>
	<li>Founder's Day Celebration</li>
	<li>SYF Arts Presentation</li>
	<li>Year-End Choir Concert</li>
	<li>Choir Music Exchange Programme</li>
</ul>
