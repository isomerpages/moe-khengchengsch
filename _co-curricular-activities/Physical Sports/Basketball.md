<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Grounding in humility. Shooting for the stars. Stronger Together'
<br>Our school basketball team strives to foster a love for the game and to
instill in our players a passion for teamwork, perseverance, and growth
mindset. Inspire and uplift others on and off the court.</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>Our mission is to develop well-rounded basketball players who have both
the skills to excel on the court and the character and values (<strong>courage</strong>, <strong>diligence</strong>, <strong>kindness</strong>, <strong>sincerity</strong>)
to achieve in life.</p>
<p>‘One man can be a crucial ingredient on a team but one man cannot make
a team’ – <strong>Kareem Abdul Jabbar</strong>
</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>Create a safe environment where every player can learn, grow and reach
their full potential.</p>
<p>Through teachable moments and game play to instill idea of teamwork, diligence,
courage, and growth mindset</p>
<h3>Approach</h3>
<p>Specialized training for different levels.
<br>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Primary 3:</strong> Foundation training</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Primary 4 to 5:</strong> Specialized training in 3v3 strategy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Primary 5 and 6:</strong> Specialized training in 5v5 strategy</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>National School Games Competition (Zonal and National) (Junior Division)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>National School Games Competition (Zonal and National) (Senior Division)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in friendly competition with other schools​</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learning journeys</p>
</li>
</ul>
