<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>To offer students an opportunity to experience a diversity of different
team sports and activities</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>Students to learn basic skills, techniques and to have fun.</p>
<h4>Objectives</h4>
<p>Students will learn teamwork, sportsmanship and develop understanding
of a specific sport.</p>
<h4>Approach</h4>
<p>Through different sports activities and students will inculcate KCS’s
core values of kindness, courage, sincerity and diligence.</p>
<h4>Key Programmes</h4>
<p>Sports modules include:</p>
<p>Tennis</p>
<p>Soccer</p>
<p>Athletics</p>
<p>Basketball</p>
<p>Flying Disc</p>
<p>Floorball</p>
<p>Street Dance</p>
<p>Volleyball</p>
