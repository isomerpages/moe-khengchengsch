<p><strong>Visual Arts Microsite</strong>
</p>
<p>Our KCS Visual Art microsite is a one-stop information platform for parents/guardians
and students to access Visual Art related information in our school. The
content of our microsite includes the following:</p>
<p>• Our Vision, Mission, and Goals for Visual Art
<br>• Our Art Curriculum
<br>• Art Materials required for Art Lessons (P1 – P6)
<br>• Our Art Programmes that support the development of students’ interest
and talent in Visual Art (including programmes in the Art &amp; Crafts
Club)
<br>• Features of Art Events and Happenings in our School
<br>• Announcement Space on upcoming Art Competitions and Events
<br>• Virtual Art Gallery of Featured Artworks done by our students
<br>• Our Art Achievements
<br>• Art Elective Programme (AEP) &amp; Direct School Admission (DSA) – Art
<br>• Contact Information of Art Teachers and Art &amp; Crafts Club Teachers</p>
