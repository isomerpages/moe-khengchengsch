<p>“We commemorated the 75th anniversary of the Singapore Red Cross Society
under the theme "Coast to Coast, Heart to Heart - Celebrating 75 Years
of Humanity" for this year's World Red Cross Day. Emphasizing our commitment
to serve all corners of Singapore, the event honoured our interconnectedness
and meaningful contributions in Singapore through SRC’s services. Highlights
of the day included awards for excellence, member enrollment, and a special
ceremony welcoming new RCY members. What a way to celebrate 75 years of
unwavering dedication to humanity together!”</p>
