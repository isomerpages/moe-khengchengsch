<p>The KCS Choir is proud to have attained an <strong>Accomplishment Award</strong> at
the recent SYF Arts Presentation 2026. Since last year, our choir members
have worked hard to master three challenging pieces for this event.</p>
<p>Through perseverance, teamwork, and resilience, they uplift one another
and stayed committed to their goal. This achievement reflects both their
musical growth and their unity as a team. The school is immensely proud
of their well-deserved accomplishment.</p>
