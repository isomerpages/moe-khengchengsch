<p>We are pleased to share that Kheng Cheng School’s Modern Dance CCA has
been awarded <strong>Distinction</strong> at the 2026 Singapore Youth Festival
(SYF) Arts Presentation, held on 27 April 2026.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This outstanding achievement reflects the dedication, discipline, and
perseverance of our dancers, who have committed themselves wholeheartedly
over the past 10 months in preparation for this performance. We would like
to commend the following students for their hard work and resilience throughout
this journey.</p>
